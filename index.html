+

Large-scale single-cell RNA sequencing project mapping cellular heterogeneity in multiple cancer types. Identifies rare cell populations, tumor-immune interactions, and developmental trajectories.

Methods: scRNA-seq, trajectory inference, cell-cell communication analysis, spatial transcriptomics

Scale: 500,000+ cells across 20 cancer types from 300+ patients

Findings: Discovered 12 novel cancer cell states and their role in treatment resistance

Drug Resistance Mechanisms

+

Systematic investigation of molecular mechanisms underlying acquired resistance to targeted therapies. Combines CRISPR screens, drug perturbation experiments, and computational modeling.

Methods: Genome-wide CRISPR screening, proteomics, network analysis, mathematical modeling

Focus: EGFR, ALK, and BRAF inhibitor resistance in lung and melanoma cancers

Results: Identified 25 resistance genes, 8 validated for therapeutic targeting
