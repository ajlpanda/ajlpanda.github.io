üìä Research Focus Areas

Drug Response Modeling

Goal: Develop accurate predictive models for chemotherapy and targeted therapy responses.

Methods: Ensemble machine learning (Random Forest, XGBoost, Neural Networks), feature selection, cross-validation

Data: GDSC, CCLE, TCGA datasets with 1000+ cell lines and patient samples

Results: 85% accuracy in predicting drug sensitivity with validated clinical outcomes

Biomarker Discovery

Goal: Identify genomic and transcriptomic biomarkers predictive of drug response.

Methods: Differential expression analysis, SHAP feature importance, pathway enrichment

Data: RNA-seq, whole exome sequencing, mutation profiles from 2,500+ tumors

Results: Discovered 50+ novel predictive biomarkers for targeted therapies

Precision Oncology

Goal: Enable personalized treatment selection based on individual patient profiles.

Methods: Multi-omics integration, clinical outcome modeling, decision support systems

Data: Prospective patient cohorts with longitudinal treatment data

Results: 30% improvement in progression-free survival in validation cohort

Drug Combination Strategies

Goal: Predict synergistic drug combinations for enhanced therapeutic efficacy.

Methods: Network pharmacology, synergy scoring, combination screening analysis

Data: High-throughput combination screening across 100+ drug pairs

Results: Identified 15 synergistic combinations now in clinical trials
